Privacy Policy

Fifteen — Picture Puzzle  ·  Last updated: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Fifteen Picture Puzzle app ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs.
  • We do not use analytics tools or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not collect the photos you use in the puzzle. All image processing happens entirely on your device.
  • We do not have servers. No data is transmitted off your device.

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using your device's standard storage:

  • Game history — your solve times and move counts.
  • Best scores — your personal best records.

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

3. Photos and Camera

The App allows you to upload photos from your device's photo library to use as puzzle images. These photos are:

  • Processed entirely on your device.
  • Never uploaded to any server.
  • Never stored permanently by the App beyond your current session.

Photo library access is only used when you explicitly tap the "Upload Photo" or "Change Photo" button.

4. In-App Purchases

Any purchases made within the App are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy at apple.com/privacy for details on how Apple handles purchase data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
